# Product

## Register

product

## Users

PreventA is used primarily by HAZOP facilitators, process-safety engineers, and
consultants running long, high-concentration workshops for energy, petrochemical,
infrastructure, and process-industry clients. Senior facilitators need speed and
traceability; junior engineers need structured guidance that helps them avoid missing
credible scenarios without presenting AI output as an engineering decision.

## Product Purpose

PreventA records HAZOP and LOPA studies as structured, reusable knowledge. It reduces
repetitive worksheet entry, retrieves relevant causes, consequences, and safeguards
from controlled sources, and produces client-ready reports without manual
spreadsheet-to-document work. Success means a facilitator can complete and deliver a
real customer study in PreventA, with all sensitive data remaining on-premises.

## Brand Personality

Precise, composed, accountable. The product should feel like a dependable engineering
instrument: calm under workshop pressure, explicit about evidence, and direct about
what requires human review.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS dashboards dominated by decorative metrics and empty cards.
- Consumer-style AI chat interfaces that hide sources or imply autonomous decisions.
- Legacy desktop toolbars with unlabeled icon rows and weak visual hierarchy.
- Dark cyber-security aesthetics, neon colors, glass effects, and decorative gradients.
- Interfaces that encode risk using color alone.

## Design Principles

1. Keep the worksheet in command. Navigation and assistance support the study table,
   not compete with it.
2. Make evidence inspectable. AI candidates always expose source, confidence, and the
   exact action that will add them to the draft.
3. Optimize for facilitation tempo. Keyboard paths, autosave feedback, sticky context,
   and compact controls reduce interruption during workshops.
4. Separate draft from decision. Generated, accepted, edited, and rejected states must
   remain visibly distinct and auditable.
5. Preserve customer ownership. Local operation and straightforward export are visible
   product qualities, not hidden infrastructure details.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. All primary workflows must work with keyboard and pointer input,
focus must remain visible, reduced motion must be respected, and risk/status meaning
must use text or icons in addition to color. Dense desktop layouts must remain usable
at 200% zoom, while narrow screens use structural disclosure rather than shrinking
the worksheet into unreadable cells.
